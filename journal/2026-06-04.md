## Decisions (and why)
- **GraphDB-only query engine.** Fidelity, not just simplicity: pyoxigraph can't hold
  the 712 MB graph, slices can't carry multi-hop ground truth, and the engines diverge
  on RDF-star (the H6 provenance type would eventually hit that).
- **`.rq` frontmatter authoritative for seed+answer, copied into docs** (user's call).
  Generator copies; `--verify` against GraphDB is the drift guard.
- **Fuzzy/semantic = Option A** (graph-anchored reference + fuzzy phrasing + LLM-judge
  scoring). The `.rq` is a label lookup, not answer derivation, so "ground truth is
  graph-derived" holds even for the type graph can't answer.
- **Emit a curated node-attribute subset, not all.** Type 01 needs a
  *text-discoverable* attribute (chromosome) to stay a tie/closed-book probe (H7); a DB
  identifier would have made it a graph win and mis-tested the type. Full property
  representation deferred.
- **Seed-bounding insight:** for 3+ hop, branching and a bounded answer can't coexist
  across hub nodes (type 04); intersection self-bounds (type 06).
