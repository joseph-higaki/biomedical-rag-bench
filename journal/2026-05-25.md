## Decisions (and why)

- **Keep the 11.3.2 pin + add a license, don't downgrade.** License-free GraphDB
  means going back to 9.x, where RDF-star support is shaky — that's the exact
  feature under test. Adding the free license is the correct path.
- **Consolidate secrets in `secrets/` (license + `.env`).** One gitignored home
  for all credentials. Cost is one explicit file-anchored `load_dotenv()` in the
  not-yet-written shared config module (`find_dotenv` won't descend into subdirs).
  Worth it for clarity / lower accidental-commit risk.
- **Parity is by query pattern, not `COUNT(*)`.** Total-count divergence across
  RDF-star engines is expected; identical pattern-query answers is the real test.
- **Make "no AI attribution in commits" deterministic, not memory-only.** Memory
  and CLAUDE.md lower the odds but don't reliably override a harness default; a
  `commit-msg` hook strips the trailer regardless of what wrote the message.
